Canadians love coffee!

Canada is the 10th largest consumer of coffee all over the world.
14.33 Lbs of coffee is consumed per person per year here.

There are four primary types of coffee beans:

  1. Arabica
  2. Robusta
  3. Liberica
  4. Excelsa

And, 4 different types of roasted coffee :

  1. Light roasts
  2. Medium roasts
  3. Dark roasts
  4. Extra Dark roasts
