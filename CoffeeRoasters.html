Canadian coffee roasters


PILOT COFFEE ROASTERS

Pilot Coffee Roasters is a Toronto based roastery. It was started by Andy and Jessie Wilkin in 2009 for passion. They offer high quality coffee as they directly trade their beans. They have received various awards and certificates for their amazing coffee and friendly baristas at their cafe. They sell a lot of variety of coffee as well as brewing gear for the perfect coffee. They ship orders over $30 across Canada.


BANFF ROASTING COMPANY

Banff Roasting Company is located in Alberta was started in 2013 by Norm O'Genski and Martin Brenner. They aim to bring it back the way coffee was enjoyed by the pioneers and our forefathers, local and fresh. They offer the freshest 100% arabica coffee products. Along with a wide variety of coffee, they also sell green beans. The customers also get a free bag of coffee on all online order. They ship all across Canada.


JAVA BLEND COFFEE ROASTERS

Situated in Nova Scotia, Java Blend Coffee Roasters was started in 1938. They are known as one of the best coffee companies in Halifax. They offer all kinds of roasts in coffee along with some really useful Brew Tech which makes making coffee fun and easy. Their coffee has been known to have an amazing taste. They ship all across the country with some delivery charges.


49th PARALLEL COFFEE ROASTERS

49th Parallel Coffee Roasters is based in Vancouver, British Columbia and have a very advanced and scientific roastery. They have a wide variety of coffee flavours and each of their coffee flavours is unique on its own. They work directly with the farmers. They ship their products all over Canada and also give free shipping for purchases over $50.
